---
name: status-report
description: Post a What I Did / What's Left / What Needs Your Call update to Slack.
---

When asked for a status update, gather: tasks completed since last update, tasks still in progress or pending, and any decision that requires human input.

Reply in exactly three sections:

**What I Did**
List each completed task as a bullet point. Be specific — name the file, endpoint, or feature.

**What's Left**
List remaining tasks in priority order.

**What Needs Your Call**
List any blockers, ambiguous requirements, or decisions the human must make before the agent can proceed. If nothing is blocking, write "Nothing blocking right now."
